FinOps: Capture Savings and Governance

Author: Vinod Kumar • Published: 2026-06-10

This article outlines a pragmatic FinOps playbook: inventory, tagging and telemetry, rightsizing, reservations and savings plans, and continuous governance via policies and automation.

Core Actions

  1. Implement an enforced tagging taxonomy.
  2. Analyze usage and rightsize underutilized compute.
  3. Automate orphaned resource reclamation with resource graph queries and runbooks.
